The Award

It was a tranquil evening. Everyone was gathered in the venue of the Business Icon Awards 2023 hosted by the Global Consortium of Science and Technology. All the businessmen and CEOs gathered are curious about who is the next winner of the “Best Branding Brilliance Award." The announcement roared in the air, and it was “Tusker Vodka.”

Tusker Vodka is the brainchild of Kasthuri Hettiarachchi, the CEO of VA Distilleries. Kasturi had compassion for wild elephants in Sri Lanka for a long time. For decades, he had been acutely aware of the struggles that elephants had endured. This is where the brand Tusker was born: to create a fund for our wild elephants' protection.

The Tusker Foundation, established alongside the launch of Tusker Vodka, serves to illuminate the purpose of its creation. With every drop of Tusker Vodka, the initiative to safeguard the majestic.

Tusker Appletini

Shake 50ml vodka, 50ml cloudy apple juice, 1 tsp lemon juice and 1 tsp maple syrup in an ice-filled shaker until the outside of the shaker feels very cold. Strain into a martini glass and garnish with a slice of apple

 Prep: 5 mins |  Serves: 1
