Toast to British Boldness

The Launch of Our Tusker Vodka in the United Kingdom

Amidst a sea of sophistication and refined taste, the grand unveiling of Tusker Vodka graced London's hallowed grounds in November '2023. A distinguished gathering of connoisseurs savored each luscious sip, as they bore witness to the opulence encapsulated within every drop. With an undeniable air of excellence, it was clear that Tusker Vodka met—and surpassed—European standards. The testimonies from those present echoed the lavish reputation of the Sri Lankan-born brand, Tusker, whose commitment to craftsmanship and purity left no room for question.
